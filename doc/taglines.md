# Taglines

Suggested title: VX6 Taglines and Brand Copy

Suggested meta description: Short, accurate, high-impact taglines for VX6, an IPv6-first decentralized peer-to-peer service network.

## Important Claim Correction

Do not use:

- "World's first completely decentralized peer-to-peer network"
- "Tor has been replaced"

Why:

- peer-to-peer networks existed long before VX6
- VX6 is not trying to replace every Tor use case
- stronger, more accurate language builds more trust

## Stronger Taglines

- IPv6-first peer networking for real services.
- Share localhost with the people who need it.
- Decentralized services, direct by design.
- Private networks for real apps, not just websites.
- Bootstrap once. Run peer to peer after that.
- A faster path for private apps, internal tools, and direct services.
- Built for peer-to-peer teams, not central gatekeepers.

## Best Homepage Tagline Options

### Option 1

VX6 is an IPv6-first decentralized peer network for real services.

### Option 2

Share localhost across peers. Keep control at the edge.

### Option 3

Private service access without the usual infrastructure sprawl.

## Safer "Tor" Positioning

Use this angle:

VX6 is not a Tor clone. It is a better fit for direct services, internal tools, peer applications, and localhost-to-localhost workflows.

Short versions:

- Built for workloads Tor was never designed for.
- A faster path for private services and team networks.
- Better for apps, tools, and direct service access.

## One-Line Brand Statements

- VX6 turns local services into peer-to-peer infrastructure.
- VX6 makes localhost portable across a decentralized IPv6 network.
- VX6 gives teams a direct network for services, tools, and collaboration.
